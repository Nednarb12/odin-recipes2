Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

In this sweet American sandwhich we have elements of flavor that defy it's simplistic construsction. It has been a favorite in many lunchrooms in the K-12 world as well as a quick snack in the adult working world, due to it's preservation and small list of ingredients. Lets learn how to perfect it!

List of ingredients:

  • Peaunt Butter. This normally can be found in the crunchyor creamy variety
  • Jelly. Normally found in the grape variety, there are many other fruity flavors. Jam is a popular substitute.
  • Two Bread Slices. This is traditionally made with white bread, however this is also flexible, perhaps avoid the savory breads though!

Now that we have our ingredients on the counter, let's look at some options we have on how to create your entree!

Preperation:

  1. The first step is the most crucial, we need to decide if we want a cold or warm entree. If cold we can skip to step 3, if warm please proceed to step 2.
  2. Warm Entree: Place both slices of bread into toaster, use desired setting.
  3. Using a butterknife, spread peanut butter evenly onto one side of bread slice.
  4. Clean butterknife with clean wet towel, and then spread jelly evenly on one side of another slice of bread.
  5. If you had toasted your bread, you may like to keep the bread slices separately prepared, if that is the case then Bon Apetit! Otherwise, place both slices togther so that the dry side of the bread is facing outward.
  6. You have now created a culinary masterpiece. Enjoy!
